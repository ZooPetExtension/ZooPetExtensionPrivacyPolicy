Privacy Policy for ZooPetExtension

Effective Date: 12/3/2024

Overview

ZooPetExtension is a fun and interactive virtual pet extension for your browser. This extension allows you to select a pet and interact with it on the Google homepage. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your data when you use this extension.

Data Collection

ZooPetExtension does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) or transmit any data to external servers.

Local Storage

The extension stores a small amount of data locally in your browser’s localStorage to remember your selected pet. This is only used to ensure that your pet choice persists across browser sessions. This data is not shared with third-party services, and you can delete it by clearing your browser’s local storage or uninstalling the extension.

Data Use

The only data used by this extension is related to your selected pet (stored in localStorage). This data is used solely for the functionality of the extension and does not involve any external servers or user tracking.

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data or track any usage patterns or browsing history. The data collected (selected pet) is stored locally and does not leave your device.

Third-Party Services

ZooPetExtension does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any changes will be reflected in this document, and the “Effective Date” at the top of the page will be updated accordingly. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at ZooPetExtension@gmail.com.
